Africas' best 7 tourist places

Giza Plateau, Cairo - Egypt

The Great Sphinx of Giza is a limestone statue of a reclining sphinx, a mythical creature with the head of a human, and the body of a lion.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Indira Indian restaurant' is one of its kind. Their vegetarian and vegan curries are simply delicious and give you a mouthwatering experience.

Entry: General entry to Giza Plateau: 200 EGP for adults, 80 EGP for students. Also, prices vary for diferent activities.

In & Around Cairo - Egyptian Museum, Coptic Cairo, and Khan El Khalili bazaar

Best Time to Visit: The best time varies and depends on seasons. The Giza is open every day from 8AM to 5PM during winter (October to March) and from 7AM to 6PM during summer (April to September).

Table Mountain - Cape Town

Cape Town is a city nestled between mountains and the sea, celebrated for its picturesque natural beauty and rich history. Table Mountain is an iconic landmark offering hiking trails or a cable car ride to the summit for spectacular views.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Cocoa'- Offers a wide menu with plenty of vegetarian options, located at several spots.

Entry: Tickets are required to go to the cable car and the prices vary.

In & Around the Cape Town - Clifton and Camps Bay Beaches, Victoria & Alfred Waterfront, Robben Island, Signal Hill and the Noon Gun, The Old Biscuit Mill and the Boulders Bay.

Best Time to Visit: From late January to late April.

Masai Mara National Reserve - Kenya

Kenya is a country renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, diverse wildlife, and rich cultural heritage. It offers a plethora of experiences ranging from safari adventures in the Masai Mara to relaxing beach holidays along the Swahili Coast.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Chowpaty'- Specializes in vegetarian Indian cuisine with a full vegetarian menu, including South and North Indian dishes, pizza, and some Chinese options.

Entry: Tickets for national parks and reserves vary, with prices often higher for non-residents of Kenya.

In & Around the Kenya - Amboseli National Park, Lake Nakuru National Park, Tsavo National Park and Diani Beach.

Best Time to Visit: During the dry seasons, from June to October and from December to March, when wildlife viewing is at its prime.

Marrakesh - Morocco

It is known for its rich culture, historic cities, stunning landscapes, and vibrant souks. With its mix of Arab, Berber, and European cultural influences, Morocco offers a unique travel experience.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Earth Café' - Marrakech's only exclusively vegan and vegetarian café, offering fresh and healthy dishes with ingredients grown on the owner's farm.

Entry: Tickets for various attractions will vary, and it's recommended to check the latest prices online or at the venue.

In & around Morocco - Fes, Chefchaouen, Merzouga and Casablanca.

Best Time to Visit: During the spring (March to May) and fall (September to November) when the weather is warm but not overly hot.

La Digue- Seychelles

La Digue, one of the most serene and picturesque islands in the Seychelles archipelago, is known for its tranquil atmosphere, stunning beaches, and traditional way of life.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Le Relax Beach House' is a great choice. This restaurant offers a variety of delicious vegetarian options that cater to different tastes, allowing you to enjoy local flavors in a beautiful setting.

Commute: Tourists can hire a bi-cycle and travel locally.

In & Around La Digue - Anse Source D'Argent, Mahe Islands, Grand Anse, Praslin Islands, Activities such as Snorkeling and Diving

Best Time to Visit: The best time to visit La Digue is during the months of April, May, October, and November.

Mauritius Island - East Africa

Mauritius, an island nation in the Indian Ocean, is a mosaic of stunning landscapes. The island's rich history is a blend of European, African, Indian, and Chinese influences, reflected in its cuisine, architecture, and traditions.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Sattvik' in Port Louis is a fantastic option. This restaurant serves a variety of delicious vegetarian Indian dishes, offering a taste of authentic flavors in a comfortable setting.

Best places around Mauritius - Le Morne Braban, Black River Gorges National Park, Île aux Cerfs, Pamplemousses Botanical Garden and Casela World of Adventures.

Best Time to Visit: From May to December when the weather is cool, dry, and sunny.

Tanzania

Tanzania, located in East Africa, is a country of vast natural beauty and incredible biodiversity. Known for its sprawling savannahs, majestic wildlife, and the towering Mount Kilimanjaro, Tanzania offers some of the most iconic African experiences.  

Vegetarian Eatery: Recommended place is "Green Garden Restaurant' in Arusha, which offers a variety of vegetarian dishes that blend local flavors with international cuisine.

Best places around Tanzania - Mount Kilimanjaro, Serengeti National Park, Ngorongoro Crater, Tarangire National Park and Zanzibar Islands.

Best Time to Visit:  
For climbing Mount Kilimanjaro, the best months are typically January to March and June to October.
For wildlife viewing in the Serengeti, the dry season from late June to October is ideal.
