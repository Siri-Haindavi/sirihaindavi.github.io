Europes' best 7 tourist places

Paris - Italy

Paris, often referred to as "The City of Light," is a haven for lovers, artists, and dreamers. Iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Notre-Dame Cathedral stand as testaments to its architectural grandeur.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Le Potager du Marais', a cozy restaurant, nestled in the Marais district, offers a delightful array of French vegetarian cuisine, with many vegan options as well.

Entry: Regarding the Eiffel Tower, the ticket prices vary depending on the level you wish to visit and your age group.

In & Around Paris - Venice, Rome, Disneyland Paris, Fontainebleau, Giverny and Versailles

Best Time to Visit: The suitable time to visit Paris is typically during the spring (April to June) or fall (September to November).

Zurich - Switzerland

Switzerland, known for its stunning alpine landscapes, is a fusion of nature's majesty and cultural diversity. It's a land where serene lakes reflect the sky, and cities blend historical charm with modern innovation  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Haus Hiltl' in Zurich, the world's oldest continuously open vegetarian restaurant, is an excellent choice that blend traditional Swiss flavors with international cuisine, in a buffet-style setting.

Entry: One popular tourist attraction in Switzerland is the Jungfraujoch, often referred to as the "Top of Europe." Ticket prices vary depending on the season and your departure point.

In & Around Zurich - Lucerne, Interlaken, Geneva, Bern and Kerns.

Best Time to Visit: The best time to visit Switzerland largely depends on your interests. It is the must visit place throughout the year.

Amsterdam - Netherlands

Amsterdam, the vibrant capital of the Netherlands, is renowned for its artistic heritage, elaborate canal syste and a variety of flower markets. It is also known for celebrating Bachelorette parties.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'De Bolhoed' is a great option. Located in the picturesque Jordaan district, this cozy café offers a variety of organic vegetarian dishes.

In & Around the Amsterdam - The Hague, Rotterdam, The Rijksmuseum, Vondelpark, Keukenhof Gardens, Zaanse Schans and Giethoorn.

Best Time to Visit: During the late spring (April to May) or early fall (September to October)

Budapest - Hungary

The enchanting capital of Hungary, is divided by the majestic Danube River into two distinct parts: Buda, with its romantic, hilly terrain and historical sites, and Pest, known for its vibrant urban energy and cultural landmarks.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Napfényes Restaurant and Pastry Shop' is a delightful choice. Located in the heart of the city, it serves a variety of delicious Hungarian and international vegetarian and vegan dishes, all made with organic ingredients.

Things to do: Explore the Castle Distric, Relax in Thermal Baths, Enjoy the Ruin Bars, Discover the Jewish Quarter and many more!

In & around Budapest - The Buda Castle, the Danube Bend, a region of peaks and river towns like Visegrád, Esztergom, and Szentendre.

Best Time to Visit: during the spring (April to June) or fall (September to November). During these seasons, the weather is mild, and the city is less crowded.

Iceland - Nordic Isands

Iceland, a Nordic island nation, is a realm of stark contrasts and natural wonders. It's a land where fire meets ice, with its dramatic landscapes comprising volcanoes, geysers, hot springs, and lava fields  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Gló' in Reykjavik is an excellent choice. Renowned for its healthy, organic food, Gló offers a range of vegetarian dishes, all prepared fresh!

In & Around Nordic Islands - Norway, Greenlands, Finland, The Blue Lagoon, The Golden Circle, Jökulsárlón Glacier Lagoon and The Northern Lights

Best Time to Visit: The best time to visit Iceland really depends on what you want to experience. However, for warmer weather and access to most tourist attractions, the summer months (June to August) are ideal.

Santorini - Greece

Santorini, a gem in the Aegean Sea, is one of the most iconic Greek islands, famed for its dramatic views, stunning sunsets, and white-washed buildings with blue domes.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Tranquilo' in Perissa is a highly recommended spot. This beachside restaurant offers a variety of fresh, organic vegetarian and vegan dishes, with a laid-back atmosphere.

In & around Santorini and Greece- Athens, Oia Village, Ancient Thera, Red Beach, Fira to Oia Hike, Wine Tours and Museum of Prehistoric Thera

Best Time to Visit: During the late spring (April to early June) and early fall (September to early October). During these periods, the weather is warm.

Lisbon - Portugal

Lisbon, the sun-kissed capital of Portugal, is a city of undeniable charm. It is known for its rich history, vibrant nightlife, and world-renowned Fado music.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Ao 26 - Vegan Food Project' is a great choice. This restaurant offers a creative array of vegan and vegetarian dishes, making it a favorite among locals and visitors alike.

In & Around Lisbon - Jerónimos Monastery, Belém Tower, Castelo de São Jorge, Oceanário de Lisboa and Alfama District.

Best Time to Visit: Either in the spring (March to May) or fall (September to November). During these seasons, the weather is pleasant and less crowded.
