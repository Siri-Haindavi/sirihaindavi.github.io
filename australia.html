Oceanias' best 7 tourist places

Great Barrier Reef - Queensland

The Great Barrier Reef, located off the coast of Queensland, Australia, is the world's largest coral reef system. It's renowned for its breathtaking underwater scenery, vibrant marine life, and clear, warm waters, making it a dream destination for snorkelers and divers.  

Vegetarian Eatery: Queensland's coastal areas near the Great Barrier Reef, such as 'Cairns or Port Douglas', offer various dining options, including vegetarian and vegan eateries.

Entry: Access to the Great Barrier Reef itself is free as it is one of the seven wonders. However, you'll typically need to book tours or activities through registered operators, which vary in price.

In & Around Queensland - Cairns, a gateway to the reef, The Daintree Rainforest and Whitsunday Islands.

Best Time to Visit: During the Australian winter, from June to October.

Sydney - Australia

Sydney, the vibrant capital of New South Wales in Australia, is famous for its iconic Sydney Opera House, picturesque harbour, stunning beaches, and a dynamic cultural scene.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Yellow in Potts Point' is a popular bistro known for its creative vegetarian dishes like tempura Jerusalem artichoke and liquorice bread.

Entry: Sydney is a city of free exploration, but some attractions, like the Sydney Tower Eye or Taronga Zoo, require admission fees.

In & Around the Sydney - Bondi Beach, Blue Mountains, Hunter Valley, Manly Beach and Royal Botanic Garden.

Best Time to Visit: During the Australian spring (September to November) or autumn (March to May).

Wellington - New Zealand

Wellington, the capital city of New Zealand, is known for its vibrant arts scene, picturesque natural harbor, and diverse culinary offerings.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Higher Taste Hare Krishna', on Customhouse Quay offers a spiritual dining experience with a variety of vegan and vegetarian dishes

Entry: It is a place of free exploration however, tickets are needed for some of the attractions and the prices vary.

In & Around the Wellington - Zealandia Ecosanctuary, Wellington Cable Car and Botanical Garden, Cuba Street and Te Papa Museum.

Best Time to Visit: During the summer months from December to February when the weather is warm and sunny, perfect for enjoying outdoor activities and festivals.

Fiji - South Pacific

Fiji, an archipelago in the South Pacific, is renowned for its stunning beaches, lush landscapes, and vibrant culture. It's a fantastic destination for those seeking a tropical getaway.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'The Botanist' in Lyall Bay is a great choice for beachside dining with an array of vegetarian and vegan options.

Entry: Tickets for various attractions will vary, and it's recommended to check the prices online based on the activities.

In & around Fiji - Trekking through Sigatoka Sand Dunes National Park, Kava Ceremony, Kula Wild Adventure Park, Great Astrolabe Reef and Beqa Lagoon

Best Time to Visit: During the fall, particularly from April to June. May is ideal fo all the summer activities.

Melbourne - Australia

Melbourne, Australia is a vibrant and culturally rich city known for its diverse culinary scene, arts, and sports. It's a city where history meets contemporary innovation, offering something for everyone.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Patsy's' is an all-vegetarian wine bar in Melbourne celebrating Mediterranean techniques and local produce.

Entry: Free exploring! However, tickets are required for activities and the prices vary.

In & Around Melbourne - Melbourne Cricket Ground, Royal Botanical Gardens, National Gallery of Victoria, Federation Square and Great Ocean Road.

Best Time to Visit: During the fall (March to May) and spring (September to November) seasons.

Bay of Islands - New Zealand

The Bay of Islands in New Zealand is a stunning subtropical micro-region known for its breathtaking beauty and abundant water activities.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Terra' restaurant offers a wide range of vegetarian dishes and mouthwatering experiences.

Entry: There are many activities at a price and they vary depending on citizens and foreigners.

Best places around Bay of Islands - Cruising and Sightseeing, Historic Russell, Waitangi Treaty Grounds, Water activities, Hiking and Wildlife.

Best Time to Visit: The Bay of Islands can be enjoyed year-round due to its mild climate. However, the summer months (December to February) are particularly popular for enjoying the beaches and water-based activities to the fullest.

Fiordland National Park - New Zealand

Fiordland National Park in New Zealand is a breathtaking natural wonder, recognized as part of the UNESCO World Heritage site, Te Wāhipounamu (Place of the Greenstone).  

Vegetarian Eatery: Recommended place is "Green Garden Restaurant' in Arusha, which offers a variety of vegetarian dishes that blend local flavors with international cuisine.

Entry: Generally, there are no entry tickets required to access Fiordland National Park itself. However, specific activities like cruises, guided walks, and some attractions within the park may have their own costs.

Best places around Fiordland - Great walks - Hiking, Cruises, Kayaking and Diving, Wildlife watching and nature activities.

Best Time to Visit: During the late spring to early summer months of November to February.
