North Americas' best 7 tourist places

Hawaii - USA

Hawaii is a tropical paradise known for its stunning beaches, volcanic landscapes, and rich Polynesian culture. Each island offers unique experiences, from Maui's beaches to the Big Island’s volcanoes.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Peace Café' in Honolulu is a great spot for vegetarian cuisine. Their dishes are simply delicious and give you a mouthwatering experience.

In & Around Hawaii - Hawaii Volcanoes National Park, Pearl Harbor National Memorial, Waikiki Beach, Haleakala National Park

Best Time to Visit: Mid-April to early June and September to mid-December for good weather and fewer crowds.

Niagara - Canada

Famous for the majestic Niagara Falls, this region offers boat tours, observation decks, and parks. The city itself has attractions like casinos and theaters.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Rise Above' in Niagara Falls offers creative vegan dishes. Niagara has many Indian restaurants as well which offers plenty of vegetarian dishes. Food and Vibe all over!

Entry: Free to view, but boat tours like the Maid of the Mist charge, Niagara's Fury and other activies are for a charge and the ticket prices and packages vary.

In & Around the Niagara - Toront, Elora, Pickering, Niagara Falls and CN Tower.

Best Time to Visit: June to August for the best views of the falls.

Las Vegas - USA

Known as the "Entertainment Capital of the World," Las Vegas is famous for its vibrant nightlife, centered around 24-hour casinos and other entertainment options.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'VegeNation' is a popular spot, offering a variety of creative vegetarian and vegan dishes.

Entry: Tickets for clubs are required and the prices vary depending on individuals. Nightlife in the downtown streets is extremely lively and free of cost.

In & Around the Vegas - Grand Canyon, Lake Tahoe, The Strip, Bellagio Fountains, High Roller Observation Wheel.

Best Time to Visit: March to May and September to November when the weather is pleasant.

Arizona - USA

Known for the Grand Canyon, Arizona features stunning desert landscapes, Native American history, and vibrant cities like Phoenix and Tucson.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Green New American Vegetarian' in Phoenix is highly recommended for its mouthwatering dishes and perfect place to have a vegetarian meal.

Entry: Tickets for various attractions will vary, and it's recommended to check the latest prices online or at the venue.

In & around Arizona - Grand Canyon National Park, Lake Havasu, Antelope Canyon and Horse show bend.

Best Time to Visit: Spring (March to May) and fall (September to November).

Alaska - USA

Known for its rugged beauty, Alaska offers vast wilderness areas, glaciers, and wildlife. It’s a destination for outdoor activities like hiking, kayaking, and wildlife viewing. It is a destination to view the beauty of Northern lights.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Middle Way Cafe' in Anchorage offers great vegetarian options with flavourful dishes and fresh ingredients. The ambience and vibe is plesant which leaves a standard for all the tourists to visit the place.

In & Around Alaska - Sitka, North Pole, Denali National Park, The Northern lights and Glacier Bay National Park

Best Time to Visit: June to August for warmer weather and outdoor activities.

Alberta - Canada

Known for its stunning natural landscapes, including Banff and Jasper National Parks. Alberta boasts mountains, lakes, and wildlife, as well as vibrant cities like Calgary and Edmonton.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'The Coup' in Calgary is a popular choice for vegetarian options. They offers a wide variety of dishes with a delightful experience.

Best places around Alberta - Banff National Park, Jasper National Park, Lake Louise, Lake Moraine, Mountains and parks

Best Time to Visit: July and August for summer activities, winter for skiing.

California - USA

California offers diverse experiences, from the beaches of Los Angeles and San Francisco’s Golden Gate Bridge to the vineyards of Napa Valley and the wilderness of Yosemite National Park.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Gracias Madre' in Los Angeles offers delicious organic Mexican vegan cuisine.

Best places around California - Golden Gate Bridge, Yosemite National Park, Disneyland Resort, Napa Valley, Santa Monica Tier, Death Valley National Park, Palm Springs, Carmel by the sea and Big Sur.

Best Time to Visit: Spring and fall for mild weather.
