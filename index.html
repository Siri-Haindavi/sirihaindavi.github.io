African Aura

Africa comprises 54 countries. It is the hottest continent and home of the world's largest desert, the Sahara, occupying the 25% of the total area of Africa and about 15% of the world's total population live in Africa!

Algeria is the largest country. The most populous country in Africa, however, is Nigeria, with more than 219 million people, but the country is only a third of the size of Algeria. Seychelles is the smallest Country, which is an archipelago (nation of islands) in the Indian Ocean. On the African mainland, the smallest country is The Gambia. The Nile is the longest river in the world and passes through eleven countries. Highest Mountain is Kilimanjaro in Tanzania.

From the pyramids of Egypt to the savannahs of the Serengeti, Africa pulsates with life and history. Amidst this backdrop, our platform guides you to vegetarian havens where you can indulge in the rich, spice-laden flavors of African cuisine. (Enter the world of Africa)

Antartic Awe

Antarctica is an ice covered continent surrounded by the Southern Ocean. Almost all of the continent's land is covered by a thick layer of ice. Antarctica is the least populated continent. There are no residents living permanently here. People are mainly based there for one year to live and work in the research stations. The research stations are scattered all around the continent.

Whales, and seals live in the Southern Ocean surrounding Antarctica. The Emperor penguins are the only penguin species breeding on Antarctica. It's a beautiful scenic place to see in Summer(December). About 40,000 tourists from around the world visit Antarctica every year on polar cruises and exploration trips.

Venture into the untouched wilderness of Antarctica. While life thrives primarily in the oceans here, we connect you with cruises that offer exquisite vegetarian dining, ensuring that your adventure is remarkable in every aspect. (Embark on the Antarctic Experience )

Asia's Essence

Asia includes 50 countries, and it is the most populated continent, the 60% of the total population of the Earth live here.

China is the largest country by population followed by India. Smallest is Maldives. Highest mountain is Mount Everest. This mountain is located in the Himalayan mountain range and the highest peak is 8,849 m/ 29,035 ft high. The Himalayas between Nepal and China are the world's highest mountains. Two of the Asian countries also share part of their territory on the European continent; these countries are Russia and Turkey.

From megacities to remote islands, Asia's top travel spots offer a hearty dose of adventure, ancient history and culture. As the largest continent, Asia is a tapestry of cultures. We navigate you through bustling markets and tranquil temples to discover vegetarian delights, from India's aromatic curries to Japan's Zen-inspired shojin ryori. (Discover the Mysteries of Asia)

Oceanic Odyssey

Oceania is in a continental group consisting of 14 countries and includes Pacific islands and Australia. The largest island of Oceania is Australia and the most populous city is Sydney. The highest mountain on land is Mount Cook in New Zealand.

The most diverse landscape will be found in Australia and New Zealand due to their size and the varied climatic regions in these countries. Oceania is home to many animal species. In Australia, the largest country of the continent, you will find Kangaroos most often. Australia's national sport is cricket. Most popolar tourist attractions in Australia are Great Barrier Reef, Kakadu National Park, Sydney Opera House, Uluru and the red centre or outback.

Explore the diverse ecosystems of Australia, from coral reefs to arid deserts. Our curated list includes vegetarian-friendly eateries where you can recharge with nutritious and delicious Aussie-style veggie dishes. (Unveil the Wonders of Oceania)

European Charm

Europe is considered to be the wealthiest and richest continent. There are 50 countries in Europe with a total of more than 742 million people living on the continent in 2023. The European part of Russia covers more area than any other country. Vatican City is the smallest country not only in Europe, but also in the world.

According to the World Happiness Report from 2018 - 2020, the World's happiest people live in Finland - and they won for the last three years in a row! So why wait, It's time to go visit it immediately. Five out of the top ten tourist destinations in the world are located in Europe. France is the number 1 tourist destination in the world with about 90 million international tourist arrivals per year.

Europe's charm lies in its history, architecture, and a multitude of cuisines. We bring you a selection of the continent's best vegetarian dining, from the trattorias of Italy to the bistros of France. (Explore the Richness of Europe)

North Americas' Allure

There are 23 countries in North America and 9 dependencies with a total of more than 590 million people living on the continent. Largest Country is Canada. The country is covering almost half the North American continent's land area. Saint Kitts and Nevis Island is the smallest country with only about 54,000 inhabitants. This island country is located in the Caribbean Sea.

Highest Mountain is the Denali in Alasaka/USA. The most populous city in North America is Mexico City. The most popular attractions include New York City which is also called the 'Big Apple'/USA for museums and shopping, California/USA for amazing beaches for surfing, Las Vegas/USA, the casino paradise with lots of popular shows and entertainment options for the whole family.

Journey through North America's vast landscapes and melting pot of cultures. Our recommendations will take you to celebrated vegetarian spots where innovation meets fresh, local produce. (Adventure into North America)

South American Splendors

There are 12 countriesin South America and 3 dependencies with a total of more than 433 million people living on the continent. Largest Country is Brazil. The country is covering more than half the continent's landmass. Suriname is the smallest country. The country is one of the 10 most sparsely populated countries in the world.

Longest River is the Amazon River. Aconcagua in Argentina is the highest mountain. There are two landlocked countries in South America: Paraguay and Bolivia are located in the interior of the continent and have neither access to the Atlantic Ocean nor to the Pacific Ocean.

Embrace the vibrant cultures of South America. Your taste buds will dance to the rhythm of vegetarian-friendly eateries that offer everything from Brazilian feijoada to Argentinian empanadas. (Journey Through South America)
