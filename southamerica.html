South Americas' best 7 tourist places

Salar de Uyuni - Bolivia

Situated in the Andes nearly 3,700 meters (12,000 feet) above sea level, Salar de Uyuni is the world’s largest salt flat. The salt flat is as flat as you can get and with the harsh terrain looks otherwordly, especially after rain when the Salar turns into a giant mirror.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Railway Cafe-Bistro', praised for its Bolivian Fusion food and focus on regional products. This cafe is well-regarded for its quinoa risotto and has a good reputation for service and atmosphere.

Entry: There's a nominal fee for entering certain areas of the salt flats. These fees are subject to change, so it's best to verify the current rates closer to your visit.

In & Around Salar - Train Cemetery, a collection of historic, rusting trains just outside Uyuni town, and the Eduardo Avaroa Andean Fauna National Reserve.

Best Time to Visit: Rainy season (December to April) for the mirror effect, while the dry season (May to November) for white salt and clear skies.

Machu Picchu - Peru

Machu Picchu, situated in the Andes Mountains of Peru, is one of the most iconic archaeological sites in the world. It's an ancient Incan city known for its well-preserved ruins and breathtaking views above the Urubamba River valley.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Green House Organic' Concept, located in Aguas Calientes, the town closest to Machu Picchu. This restaurant is known for its Peruvian and healthy cuisine, offering vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options.

Entry: Tickets are required and the prices vary. Also, these should be purchased in advance due to visitor limits.

In & Around the Machu - Inca Trail, a famous trek that leads to Machu Picchu, and other Incan sites like Huayna Picchu and the Sun Gate.

Best Time to Visit: Dry season from May to September is generally recommended for clearer skies and better hiking conditions.

Tayrona National Park - Colombia

Tayrona National Park in Colombia is a magnificent natural reserve known for its stunning beaches, lush rainforests, and rich biodiversity.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Casa Tayrona Los Naranjos' is highly recommended, known for its excellent vegetarian food selection. Offers a variety of healthy and vegetarian dishes like vegetable wraps, vegetarian sandwiches, and spring pasta with pesto.

Entry: The entrance fee for foreigners during high season is 73,500 COP. There are other packages as well!

In Tayrona - Visitors should explore iTayrona's stunning landscapes, combining lush forests, beautiful beaches, and diverse wildlife. Additionally, medical insurance costing 5,000 COP per person per day is mandatory.

Best Time to Visit: During the dry season from December to April, offering the most pleasant weather for exploration. The park also closes in February for a month.

Los Glaciares National Park - Argentina

Los Glaciares National Park in Argentina is a stunning natural reserve, renowned for its impressive glaciers, rugged mountains, and beautiful lakes.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'El Calafate', the nearest town to the park. Pura Vida Restobar is a popular choice, offering delicious home cooking with excellent vegetarian dishes

Entry: Tickets for various attractions will vary, and it's recommended to check the latest prices online or at the venue.

In & around Los Glaciares - Besides glacier trekking and hiking, you can enjoy boat tours to the Upsala and Spegazzini glaciers.

Best Time to Visit: During the Southern Hemisphere's summer months, from November to March,

Quito - Ecuador

The place where you can see the center of the earth - Quito, the capital of Ecuador, is a city rich in history and culture, nestled in the Andean foothills. It's known for its well-preserved colonial center, with buildings dating back to the 16th and 17th centuries.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Tandana' is an all-vegan restaurant offering a variety of dishes, including falafel and risotto, with a view of the Guapulo valley.

Note: Center of the Earth!

In & Around Quito - A middle of the World Monument, where you can stand on the equator line, and the TelefériQo, a cable car ride providing stunning views of the city and the surrounding Andes Mountains.

Best Time to Visit: during the dry season, from June to September, when the weather is milder and more conducive to outdoor activities and exploration.

Torres del Paine National Park - Chile

Torres del Paine National Park in Chile is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, including towering mountains, blue glaciers, and emerald lakes.  

Vegetarian Eatery: Some popular restaurants in the region include Lago Grey Bar & Restaurant and Pudeto Catamarán Dock And Cafeteria, which offer a variety of vegetarian dishes.

Best places around Chile - The Atacama Desert, Marble Caves, Cape Horn, Sky Costanera and Museum of Memory and Human Rights.

Best Time to Visit: during the Southern Hemisphere's summer months, from November to March, when the weather is more favorable for exploring the park's natural beauty.

Rio de Janeiro - Brazil

Rio de Janeiro, known for its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and iconic landmarks like the Christ the Redeemer statue and Sugarloaf Mountain.  

Vegetarian Eatery: 'Vegetariano Social Clube', a 100% vegan restaurant offers a vegan buffet daily, along with lunch, dinner dishes, and pizzas.

Best places around Brazil - From the famous Copacabana and Ipanema beaches to the Tijuca National Park, and the bustling nightlife of Lapa, there's something for everyone in this dynamic city.

Best Time to Visit: Peak tourist season is during the Southern Hemisphere's summer, from December to March.
